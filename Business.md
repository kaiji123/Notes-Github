## 2. What to Ask For When Ordering

When placing your trade order with a supplier, specify these parameters so everything fits together seamlessly:

1. **Cup Size:**
* **300 ml** (ideal for smaller spritzes/fizzes)
* **400 ml – 500 ml** (standard size for highballs/coolers loaded with ice and garnish)


2. **Cup Diameter:** Usually **Ø 95 mm** (standard industry fit).
3. **Dome Lid Style:**
* **S otvorom pre slamku** (*with hole*) for drinks like the Watermelon Cooler served with straws.
* **Bez otvoru** (*without hole*) if you prefer completely spill-proof transport until the guest reaches their destination.
